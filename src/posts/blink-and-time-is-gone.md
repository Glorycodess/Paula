---
title: Blink and Time Is Gone
date: 2026-06-05
category: Life
excerpt: She probably told herself she'd only do it for a year. A decade later, she's still there.
---

Back when I was in Junior secondary school, my mom used to give me money for transportation, but I almost never used the money because one of my neighbors who attended the same school as me always walked to school. Let's call this neighbor "Faith".

Now here's the thing, Faith would leave the house as early as 6am, so usually I had to dress up fast, go to her house, wait for her and then we'd start to walk together. All this I did because I wanted to save my extra cash 🤣

Close to my school, there was this woman who sold soft drinks, groundnuts and bananas. And Faith would always stop by her shop to help the lady bring things out before we continued walking to school.

On good days, this woman would give Faith and I extra ₦100-200 or even soft drinks — and this would always make our day. After school, we'd wave this woman goodbye as we walked back home. My guess is that she stayed there 6am-6pm every day.

You see, I've been out of Junior secondary school for a long time. "Faith" is now a big girl. She's always been older than me. She and I don't even talk anymore and we haven't seen each other for more than 5 years.

But early this year, I was in Abuja and I passed by my old secondary school on a random morning — and guess who I saw? The banana lady. Same spot, same location, selling soft drinks, groundnuts and bananas.

She probably doesn't even remember me anymore. But how could I forget her? When I saw her there it made me realize how life can happen to you if you get too comfortable.

I know her as the banana lady, but she probably had goals for herself. She probably told herself she'd only sell bananas for 1 year, and then she'd get a real life, something fancy. But a decade later, she's still there.

It's actually so sad, because this will become a lot of people's reality soon. You may not realize how dangerous it is that you're being comfortable now. You may think you still have time. All you have to do is blink, and time is gone.

Not one single person who's poor today planned to be poor. You'd think your case will be different. Sadly, until you get stubborn and force life to work, nothing will change.
